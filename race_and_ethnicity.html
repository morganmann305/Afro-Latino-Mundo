What is Race & Ethnicity?

A person's race is their biological makeup, which determines their physical appearance including skin color.

Ethnicity refers to the culture a group of people share who live in the same country, speak the same language and share similar customs.

Therefore, a person of any race can be of any ethnicity. So, people who are black or white, can both be ethnically Latino and be Cuban, for example.

Being Latino is unique because a Latino can be of any race. Just like how African Americans, white Americans and Asian Americans are all of different races, they are all ethnically American.
